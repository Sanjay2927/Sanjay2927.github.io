I'm Sanjay Narayanan Sankaranarayanan — a data analyst with a Master's in Decision Analytics (Data Science in Business Management) from VCU, graduating with a 3.7 GPA. I don't lock myself into one industry. I bring analytical thinking, structured problem solving, and clear communication to wherever the data lives — finance, operations, consulting, AI, public sector, or supply chain.

My background combines engineering fundamentals, business training across two graduate programs, and hands-on work in manufacturing analytics and AI product development. I know how to go from raw data to a recommendation a decision-maker can actually act on — and I know how to present it in a way that lands.

I'm equally comfortable building a Tableau dashboard for an executive, writing a Spark SQL pipeline for large-scale data, or structuring a strategic analysis for a consulting deliverable. The tools change. The approach doesn't.
